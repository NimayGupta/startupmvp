## Full Description (≤ 2800 characters)

Stop guessing which discounts work.

Discount Optimizer uses Bayesian statistics and machine learning to find the exact discount percentage that maximizes your order rate — without giving away margin unnecessarily.

**How it works:**

1. The app analyzes your last 28 days of order history, current prices, and inventory levels.
2. Our AI engine recommends a discount percentage with a plain-English explanation of why.
3. You approve (or edit) the suggestion — an A/B experiment starts instantly.
4. Every 6 hours, the engine checks whether the discounted price is outperforming the original. When statistical significance is reached (≥ 95% Bayesian probability), the experiment concludes automatically.
5. Over time, the system learns your approval patterns and builds a Trust Score. Once your score is high enough, the Pro plan lets you switch to fully automatic mode — experiments launch and conclude without you lifting a finger.

**Key features:**

- **Bayesian A/B testing** — no fixed sample size required; experiments stop as soon as there's enough evidence
- **AI-generated explanations** — every recommendation includes a human-readable rationale
- **Thompson Sampling bandit** — learns which discount levels work best for your price range and inventory tier
- **Trust Score** — a transparent measure of the system's confidence in your store's data quality
- **Auto-Approve mode** (Pro) — fully autonomous discount optimization while you focus on your business
- **Kill switch** — stop any experiment instantly from the dashboard
- **Zero configuration** — connects to your Shopify catalog automatically on install
